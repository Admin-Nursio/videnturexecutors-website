Intelligent Hiring Funnel

Four steps. Full picture.

Our adaptive pipeline evaluates intent, competency, and context — then routes every candidate to the right outcome automatically.

01
🎥
Video Screening
Identity & intent validation via WebRTC. Available on WhatsApp, Web, and App. Optimised for low-bandwidth rural connections.
02
🎙️
AI Voice Interview
5–8 adaptive, role-based questions in Odia. Fine-tuned Whisper model handles multi-dialect speech at 85–90% accuracy.
03
⚖️
Hybrid Evaluation
Final Score = 70% AI + 30% Human review across four dimensions: technical accuracy, relevance, communication, behaviour.
≥ 70% → Placement 60–69% → Training < 60% → Reattempt
04
📚
Training Bridge
Borderline candidates auto-enroll in Odia-first micro-learning via Moodle LMS. On completion they re-enter the funnel automatically — nobody is lost.
